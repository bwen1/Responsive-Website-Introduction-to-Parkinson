Tired of technology? Tired of noise and smog? Come take a stroll around Parkinson, the perfect contrary to your technologically enviroment!

Come explore the trail surrounding the lake and dive into the woods. Not only filled with wildlife, but also parks and exercise equipments for your daily cardio needs and tennis courts for a ball game.

Joggers in the mornings, quiet in the evenings, depending on your tastes you can come at different times for a nice relaxing stroll with the natural scenery and picture perfect sunsets you do not want to miss out on.

Along our boardwalk bush trails, you can experience the fresh natural air surrounded by the luscious trees complimented until you reach the duck pond where you have the perfect spot for a afternoon picnic.

So come on down and visit our beauitiful community, ripe with nature and wildlife, welcoming to all and perfect for your outdoor cravings.
